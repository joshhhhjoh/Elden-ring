Prep (once)

Disable Easy Anti-Cheat (EAC)
1
Open: ...\Steam\steamapps\common\ELDEN RING\Game\
2
Rename start_protected_game.exe → start_protected_game.exe.bak
3
Copy eldenring.exe and rename the copy to start_protected_game.exe

Why: You’ll launch without EAC so CE can attach safely while offline.

Launch & Mod (each session)

1
Launch Elden Ring (offline, EAC‑less).
2
Open Cheat Engine and attach to eldenring.exe.
3
Load your table: ItemGib, AddSoul, or TGA. Toggle what you need.
Quick checklist

Duping Loop (optional)

1
Drop spawned items on the ground.
2
Quit the game.
3
Restore your save backup over ER0000.sl2.
4
Launch offline again → pick up the dropped items. Repeat.

Tip: Keep two saves — CLEAN_SAVE and WORKING_SAVE. Restore CLEAN_SAVE before going online.

Going Back Online (clean exit)

1
Delete the fake start_protected_game.exe.
2
Rename your .bak back to start_protected_game.exe.
3
Restore your clean, unmodded save.
4
Set Steam back online and launch normally.
This controls the sticky warning below.

Troubleshooting

Game won’t launch offline
  • Recheck Steam’s Go Offline (restart Steam).
  • Verify the EAC rename/copy steps in the Game folder.
CE can’t attach
  • Make sure you launched the EAC‑less executable.
  • Attach to eldenring.exe (not another process).
Going online causes issues
  • Restore original start_protected_game.exe and remove the copy.
  • Use a clean save — never a modded one.
